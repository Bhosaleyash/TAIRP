Recipe 1: Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook until tender.
  3. In a separate pan, cook ground beef with onion and garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the ground beef mixture and simmer.
  5. Drain spaghetti and mix with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot.

Recipe 2: Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar.
  3. Add vanilla extract and eggs; mix well.
  4. Gradually add flour and mix until well combined.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a greased baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Let cool before serving.
